She Can

She Can is a website to uplift and empower women in tech. She Can is a one stop destination for all the girls who are passionate about tech and want to pursue their career in technicalcal field . We believe that if a girl is given a chance then she will show the world that she can . As it's a very well known fact that the gender ratio is very low in the technical college, so girls over there are very much deprived with the knowledge about the opportunities they have, so to ensure that all the girls know about the opportunities which the world provides them we “She Can” is here.

Our Aim

We will keep you posted with all the scholarships, internship opportunities,mentorship programs and open source programmes which are exclusively available for Women in the Tech field. We will try our best to redirect you to the person with the best knowledge so that you can go through the process and grab the opportunity and stand in a position to show the world that no matter what but if you are given opportunities you definitely Can.

Scholarships

Scholarships for women in tech field are very much important to boost their confidence and provide them with financial assistance. Tech has always been a male dominant field, so women are still skeptical about taking it as a career. Scholarships will open up the world of tech for them, and it will show them how many opportunities women are having in technicalcal field, therefore women would persue it more and the gender bias which is there in tech community could be eradicated. So to ensure the diversity many companies and organisation provide scholarships to women. A few of it is listed here:-

Internships and programs

Many companies and organisation provide internships specially for women so that they can ensure the diversity in the company and organisation. Many organisation offer mentorship programs where women can learn explore and make their goal even strong to be in the tech field, like wise the tech giants provide with internship opportunities for the women who are passionate to join the tech industry, build identity an prosper in life. A few of it is listed here:-

Outreachy

Outreachy is a program that organizes three-month paid internships with free and open-source software projects for people who are typically underrepresented in those projects.

Know More
Google STEP

STEP (Student Training in Engineering Program), is a 12-week internship for first and second-year undergraduate students with a passion for computer science.

Know More
Microsoft Codess

Codess is a global platform by Microsoft to encourage women in the field of technology. It enables female engineering students to receive mentorship from Microsoft Employees.
